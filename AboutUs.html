Our Story


Q1 was founded on the core of the Businessworld. After years of experience, we were unable to find a simple, suitable, and intuitive inventory software solution that met our needs and functional on site, despite our awareness of the potential leaks of such software.

Our research revealed that the Key to a successful inventory management system involves the right software combined with the appropriate storage setup, strategy and Equipment. As a result, we took matters into our own hands.

With our experience and well-tested solutions storage setup, we developed the Q1 which includes a cloud-based inventory software solution that is easy to use and has all the necessary features for management, including daily orders, accurate inventory, costs, revenue center, suppliers, departments, invoicing, History and useful filters, all in a simple and clear design.

Furthermore, the package includes a storage setup with the suitable management strategy for each discrete business unit, including labels, barcodes, equipment, and team training.

Q1 has developed a system strategy that has approved meaningful impact in a very short timeframe after acquiring a deep understanding of the real challenges that almost every business has encountered.
